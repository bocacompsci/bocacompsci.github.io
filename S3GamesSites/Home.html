About Us

We are Supernova Games, an independent game development team based in Glasgow, Scotland. We were established in December 2025, originally as a small group of friends who wanted to make the kind of games we felt were missing from the industry. What started as a side project in a cramped room with too many energy drinks has grown into a proper studio with big ambitions.

Our focus is on creating games that feel alive — worlds with personality, mechanics that reward curiosity, and stories that stick with you long after you stop playing. We’re obsessed with experimenting, learning new tools, and pushing ourselves creatively with every project. Even though we’re still a young studio, we’re driven by the same passion that got us started: making games we’re genuinely proud of and building a community around them.

Supernova Games is all about collaboration, weird ideas, and the belief that small teams can make experiences that hit just as hard as the big studios.
