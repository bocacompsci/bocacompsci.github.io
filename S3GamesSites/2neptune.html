ABOUT US

Welcome to Neptune We are a small team of creators, pixelsmiths, and caffeine-fueled gamers on a mission to bring back the golden age of pulse-pounding arcade action. At Neptune, we don't just build games—we build obsession. We're talking about that addictive, "okay, just one more run" kind of magic that tests your reflexes, pushes your limits, and occasionally makes you want to high-five the ceiling. From dodging neon lasers at supersonic speeds to blasting alien invaders out of the cosmos, we pour our hearts into creating pure, unadulterated fun. We make the games we love to play, and we've built two distinct universes for you to conquer:

🚀 Space Dash Think you've got lightning-fast reflexes? Think again. Space Dash is our high-stakes, gravity-defying rhythm runner that pits your timing against a chaotic void of neon obstacles. It’s fast, it’s unforgiving, and finding your perfect flow state is an absolute rush. Every millisecond counts—can you survive the dash?

👾 Galaxy Defender (Note: Since your homepage text mentioned defending the galaxy from an extraterrestrial threat, here is a matching description for your second game!) Lock and load for the ultimate retro-modern showdown. In Galaxy Defender, you are the only thing standing between the cosmos and total alien annihilation. We’ve dialed up the intensity with waves of clever extraterrestrial enemies, explosive power-ups, and classic arcade-style shoot-'em-up action.
